Fajitas-Recipe

Odin-Recipes

Description

Fried chicken with an amazing special seasoning. Peppers, onions, grated cheese and guacamole all put together in one delicious tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 300g
  • Pepper - 2
  • Onion - 1
  • Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Fry your chicken in a pan on high heat with seasoning
  2. Chop Peppers and Onion
  3. Grate Cheese
  4. Put chopped pepper and onion into pan once chicken is done on a low heat
  5. Add to bowl from pan
  6. Fill up tortilla with all the ingredients starting with guacamole for the base and enjoy!
