Tuna-Mayo-Sweetcorn-Sandwhich-Recipe

Odin-Recipes

Description

Sanwhich with Tuna Mayonaise and Sweetcorn

Ingredients

  • Tuna in springwater - 1 can
  • Sweetcorn - 1 can
  • Mayonaise
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Open tuna can and press water out of the tuna then add to a bowl
  2. Open sweetcorn can and empty water from it and add to same bowl
  3. Add mayonaise and mix
  4. Put onto bread and enjoy!
