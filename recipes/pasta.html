Description

Pasta and bolognese sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta - Penne
  • Bolognese sauce - half a jar
  • Cheese - optional

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water half way and boil until bubbling
  2. Add salt and olive oil(optional)
  3. Put in pasta and stir until rubbery and can break apart easily
  4. Rinse out water from pot and add bolognese sauce
  5. Stir until mixed well and enjoy!
